# Alaska Rainbow Lodge sample site — image credits

This is an unaffiliated cold-outreach sample built by BlueWave Projects. All
photos below are license-clean (public domain or Creative-Commons) images
sourced from Wikimedia Commons and verified via the Commons API
(`imageinfo` / `extmetadata`). None are Alaska Rainbow Lodge's own copyrighted
photography — the final build for the lodge would use their own official
photography, branding, and logo. Credits are also surfaced on-image and in the
page's honest sample-disclaimer footer.

These are generic Bristol Bay / Alaska fly-fishing, floatplane, and wildlife
images — NOT photos taken at Alaska Rainbow Lodge itself, and NOT the lodge's
branding or logo. No identifiable guests are shown.

Notes:
- Originals were downloaded with curl (identifying UA + contact email) and
  converted to optimized WebP (`cwebp` q82; oversized originals downsampled
  with `sips` first — hero to 2000px wide, others to 1200–1600px on the long
  edge). Source JPGs were removed after conversion.
- No fitting license-clean photo was found for the "Dick Proenneke Cabin Tour"
  add-on or the lodge's own accommodations/interiors — those sections keep
  designed SVG placeholder art with an honest "final build uses the lodge's
  own photography" note, consistent with every other tile that has no
  licensed photo behind it.
- These images depict Katmai National Park, the Kvichak River, and general
  Bristol Bay wildlife/fisheries scenery — used as stand-ins for the region,
  not represented as taken at Alaska Rainbow Lodge's property.
