# Yukon Quest 2027 sample hub — image credits & licenses

All imagery in `/public/demos/yukon/` is genuinely license-clean, sourced from
Wikimedia Commons and license-verified via the Commons API before use. These are
**generic, license-clean Alaska sled-dog / winter scenery** photos only.

None of the following were used, per the brief:
- No Iditarod-specific or Iditarod-labeled photo (the Iditarod is a *different*
  race; using its imagery would misrepresent the Yukon Quest).
- No copyrighted Yukon Quest event photo and no identifiable competitor/musher.
  (A BLM public-domain "Birch Creek WSR mushing" frame was available but was
  **rejected** because its own caption names an identifiable competitor at the
  2008 Yukon Quest — an event/competitor image, so it was not used.)
- No identifiable photos of Interior Alaska Native village residents.

Both photos used show sled dogs as cared-for athletes (a team standing/resting on
a sunlit winter trail, and a team at rest in the spruce), never distressed or as
spectacle.

CC BY-SA 2.0 images carry an on-image credit pill on the page **and** a line in
the page's SampleNote credits block. (No public-domain image was used in the
final wiring, so none needs an "uncredited" note.)

### Kept as designed SVG (not photographed)

Every other image slot on the hub keeps its designed spruce/gold woodcut SVG art
— the quick-facts tiles, the checkpoint cards, the "how to follow," "attend /
volunteer / enter," FAQ, dog-care band, and sponsor band. Those describe specific
checkpoints, communities (including Tribal Partner villages), and organizational
details; using a real photo there could misrepresent a specific place or person,
so the honest woodcut art is retained by design.

### Note

A real production build would use Yukon Quest Alaska's **own or licensed**
photography and logo in these slots. The two CC BY-SA 2.0 photos here are generic
Interior Alaska sled-dog scenery, credited on-page and in the SampleNote, used
only to make this sample read as a finished page rather than an empty shell.
