# Denali Park Road info-hub — image credits

Sample / portfolio info hub built by **BlueWave Projects** (https://bluewaveprojects.com)
at `/demos/denali-park-road`. This page is **NOT affiliated with or endorsed by
the National Park Service**. It is a visitor-information sample built on public
information; a real build would use official NPS information and licensed photography.

All images below are **U.S. National Park Service (NPS) works** sourced from
Wikimedia Commons and are in the **public domain** (works of U.S. federal
government employees created in the course of their duties). Even though public
domain requires no attribution, we credit "NPS" on-image and here as a courtesy
and for provenance. Each license was verified via the Wikimedia Commons API
(`extmetadata.LicenseShortName = "Public domain"`) on 2026-07-03.

No copyrighted photos. No identifiable people as subjects. Wildlife and landscape only.
