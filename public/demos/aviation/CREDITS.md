# Great Alaska Aviation Gathering sample hub — image credits

All photos below are license-clean (Creative-Commons) images sourced from
Wikimedia Commons and verified via the Commons API (imageinfo / extmetadata).
Each is used only as a sample placeholder in the BlueWave Projects proof build;
the final Alaska Airmen's Association build would use the organization's own
official event photography, branding, and logo. Credits are also surfaced
on-image and in the page's honest SampleNote.

These are generic Alaska general-aviation / bush-flying images — NOT copyrighted
Great Alaska Aviation Gathering event photos, and NOT the Alaska Airmen's
Association's branding or logo. No identifiable individuals are foregrounded as
subjects.

Notes:
- Originals were downloaded with curl and converted to optimized WebP (cwebp
  q80-82; hero + Lake Hood resized to <=2000 / 1200px wide). Source JPGs were
  removed after conversion.
- The "Show & Shine", "forums", "kids", and "exhibitors" tiles keep the designed
  SVG art (sky/runway/propeller motif): no fitting license-clean photo was found
  that could be shown without risking misrepresentation or copyrighted event
  imagery, so honest SVG placeholders are retained for those slots.
- These images are located in Ketchikan, Anchorage (Lake Hood), Eklutna Lake, and
  the Katmai coast — general Alaska bush-flying scenery, used as stand-ins, not
  represented as Palmer- or Gathering-specific.
