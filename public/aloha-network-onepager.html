What you get

  • Closed pocket-listing feed — visible only to founding members + paid tiers. Post listings with the privacy level you choose (anonymous relay, direct contact, network-only).
  • Adjacent-owner lookups — any parcel in Hawaii. Tap the parcel, see the four neighbors, their last sale, their lot size.
  • Permit anomaly alerts — Honolulu DPP + Maui MIPS + Hawaii County + Kauai. Real-time. Filter by your territory.
  • Adjacent ownership-change alerts — TMK-level. Often precede a sale by 6-18 months.
  • Free Property Brief subscription — weekly digest on one address of your choice. Wed 6:30am HST.
  • Direct line to the operator — feature requests, data feedback, founding-member-only access to the roadmap.

What you bring (founding tier)

One pocket listing per quarter. Could be a referral, a tip, a "my client is quietly thinking about selling, here's the situation" — anything you'd normally share with your closest 6 colleagues. Three a year. That's the entire reciprocity ask.

The data layer

Every parcel in Hawaii (384,262), every Honolulu building footprint with real heights (239,458), every address point (204,775). Compiled as TypeScript, served via API, refreshed weekly from state and county GIS endpoints. The same data foundation powers Property Brief, the Hawaii Property Lookup, and the 3D map at maps.ikenagroup.com. Two years in the making.
